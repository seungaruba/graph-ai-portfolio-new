About Me

I am a Quality Improvement and Sustainable Systems Enthusiast based in Abuja, Nigeria, with over 7 years of experience coordinating initiatives that rely on effective data usage. Currently serving as a Deputy Technical Advisor at Caritas Nigeria, I lead teams to strengthen health systems, improve clinical processes, and build capacity across state levels.

My background spans from Software Development to Public Health Management, giving me a unique perspective on how technology can solve complex systemic problems. I have successfully managed relationships with major stakeholders including the CDC and FMOH, driving data-driven advocacy and policy development.

I am now expanding my expertise into Generative AI to explore how automated workflows and LLMs can further revolutionize research and health data analysis.

Course Modules

A breakdown of the "Generative AI for Work & Research" curriculum by The GRAPH Courses.

Module 01

Websites, Docs & Presentations

Generating text responsibly, creating project websites with no-code tools, and designing clear presentations using AI assistants.

  • LLM Fundamentals
  • Responsible text generation
  • No-code web design
Module 02

AI-Automated Data Analysis

Mastering AI tools for analyzing data, manipulating files, and creating accurate, ethical automated reports using tools like Julius and Code Interpreter.

  • Automated dashboards
  • Dataset processing with Julius
  • Spreadsheet automation
Module 03

GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

Techniques for literature search, text classification, and knowledge synthesis to accelerate information gathering.

  • Literature reviews (Elicit/LiteRev)
  • Sentiment analysis
  • Knowledge mapping
Module 04

Custom GPTs & Chatbots

Building chatbots for research assistance and knowledge management using RAG systems and Botpress.

  • RAG Systems Architecture
  • Building Custom GPTs
  • Interactive chatbots
Module 05

AI for Task Automation

Strategies for automating repetitive research tasks and orchestrating app-to-app workflows.

  • Agent-based systems
  • Email response automation
  • Browser control agents
Module 06

Communication & Outreach

Creating authentic assets for sharing findings responsibly, from video abstracts to scientific figures.

  • Research summaries
  • Scientific visualization
  • Content scheduling
Module 07

Capstone Project

Collaborating to create and refine a final AI solution, tool, or presentation demonstrating responsible AI practice.

  • Project Development
  • Peer Feedback
  • Final Presentation
